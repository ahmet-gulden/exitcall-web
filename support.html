Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a call?

Tap the clock icon on the home screen, choose a character, and set the time. The call will arrive as a notification at the scheduled time.

What is Pro?

Pro removes the daily call limit and unlocks all characters. You can subscribe monthly or annually from the app.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the App Store → tap your profile → Subscriptions → ExitCall → Cancel. Apple manages all billing.

The call didn't arrive

Make sure notifications are enabled for ExitCall in Settings → Notifications. Scheduled calls require notification permission.
