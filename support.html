How do I schedule a call?

Open ExitCall, pick a character, and set a delay or schedule a specific time. The call will arrive as a real incoming call notification at the set time.

What is Pro?

Pro removes the daily call limit and unlocks all characters. You can subscribe monthly or annually from the app.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings on your device, tap your Apple ID, then Subscriptions, and select ExitCall to manage or cancel. Apple manages all billing.

The call didn't arrive

Make sure notifications are enabled for ExitCall in Settings. Scheduled calls require notification permission to work.
