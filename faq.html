Frequently Asked Questions

What is ExitCall?

ExitCall is an app that simulates realistic incoming phone calls to help you leave uncomfortable or awkward situations gracefully.

How does it work?

Set a delay or schedule a time, and ExitCall will ring your phone with a realistic incoming call. When you answer, you'll hear a voice conversation generated by text-to-speech. To anyone around you, it looks and sounds like a real phone call.

Is the call real?

No. ExitCall simulates an incoming call using VoIP technology. It does not make actual phone calls or connect to another person.

Do I need an internet connection?

An internet connection is needed to schedule calls (the server sends you a push notification at the scheduled time). The call itself works offline once it starts.

What does the free trial include?

New users get full access to all features for 3 days. After the trial, some features require a Pro subscription.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings on your device, tap your Apple ID, then Subscriptions, and select ExitCall to manage or cancel.
